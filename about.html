AMS Club

Association of MCA Students.

Aim Of The Association

The association wants to make the people technically more stronger by sharing the knowledge to them.

The association is organizing different workshops and seminars on the latest technologies as well as organizing some Non-Technical Events Inter-Computer Science And Engineering Department Students and Social Events, helping the social and technically weak people by giving them right knowledge.

Organization Overview

The club was originally established in 2001 by the Computer Science And Engineering Department, Institute Of Technologies, Nirma University.

All the students of Master of Computer Application Department are the by default member of the AMS.
